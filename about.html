About Alan Fessenden

Alan Fessenden is a writer, performer, and creative collaborator working across media. He is currently developing a trilogy of illustrated novels including Sweet Bunny, The Crystal Dragons, and a forthcoming final installment told from Adele’s perspective.

Alan’s theatrical work includes The HelpFULLS: Now and Forever, a surreal comedy about caretaking and collective memory, and he is the creator of the podcast They Just Keep Talking.

Based in New York, Alan is developing a pilot, working with clients as a consultant and story editor, and curating objects and artwork for SweetBunny.store.
