About Alan Fessenden

Alan Fessenden is a writer, director, and performer based in New York. His work blends surrealism, warmth, and humor to explore intimacy, identity, and the stories we tell ourselves.

Sweet Bunny Trilogy

Alan is the author of the upcoming trilogy beginning with Sweet Bunny, followed by The Crystal Dragons and a final book told from Adele's perspective. The trilogy is a deconstructed exploration of desire, narrative, and transformation across dreamlike landscapes.

Check out the store: sweetbunny.store

The HelpFULLS: Now and Forever

A comic and surreal play about caregiving and performance, The HelpFULLS: Now and Forever combines theatrical absurdity with emotional realism. Alan is currently preparing treatments of the piece for both stage and screen.

They Just Keep Talking (Podcast)

Alan hosts the podcast They Just Keep Talking, where he interviews artists and collaborators about creativity, process, and how we keep going. Listen to episodes here.
